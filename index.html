OUR LATEST ISSUE: CANNABIS EDITION

HONEYSUCKLE is a print and digital magazine that emphasizes diverse voices, personal testimony, and visual impact. Each quarter, our print edition explores a topical issue through a range of perspectives. Our boots-on-the-ground reporting combines input from everyday people, high-profile celebrities, and personal testimony. Our cross-generational audience is open, inclusive, and curious, united by their shared passion for knowledge and desire to dive deep into the themes that shape our time.
